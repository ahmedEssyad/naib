# Required Documents — Regardless of Legal Form

SOURCE: https://apim.gov.mr/investor-services/business-setup/
ORGANISME: APIM — One-Stop Shop for Business Registration
DATE_INDEXATION: 2026-05-17

For any business-creation application at the APIM One-Stop Shop — whatever the chosen legal form (sole proprietorship, corporate entity, GIE, or branch) — the following documents are **always required**.

## The common base for all forms

1. **Unified Business Creation Form**, duly filled in and signed.
   - Standardised document established by Decree no. 2022-169 of 17 November 2022.
   - Available at the One-Stop Shop or downloadable from the Khidmaty platform.

2. **Valid ID document of the founder(s)**:
   - Mauritanian: **national ID card**.
   - Resident foreigner: **residence card**.
   - Non-resident foreigner: **passport**.
   - The number of photocopies required varies by form (generally 4 for the main founder, 2 per partner/employee).

3. **Proof of address** (registered office):
   - **Property title**, **lease agreement**, or **occupancy certificate**, in two certified copies.
   - **Plus an electricity or water bill** in the name of the premises.
   - A Mauritanian address is **mandatory**.

4. **Administrative authorisation to operate**, **when required** by the activity.
   - Notably for: health, education, transport, finance, telecoms, security, regulated sectors.
   - Check with the One-Stop Shop whether your sector requires one.

5. **Payment of the Commercial Court registry fees**:
   - Via the **mobile-banking accounts** opened for this purpose.
   - Amount: 200 MRU (sole proprietorship) or 500 MRU (corporate entity).

6. **Copies of the ID of each declared employee** (two per employee), **if the business employs staff at creation**.

## To remember before going to the One-Stop Shop

- **Complete file = 48-hour turnaround**. Incomplete file = the clock doesn't start.
- **Bank account opened beforehand**: it's a precondition, not a later step.
- **Registry fees paid in advance** via mobile banking — no cash accepted at the counter.
- **Regulated activities**: check whether a prior authorisation is required before submitting your file.
