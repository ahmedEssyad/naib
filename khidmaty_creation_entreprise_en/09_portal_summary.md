# Official procedures.gov.mr Fiche — Setting Up a Business

SOURCE: https://procedures.gov.mr/fr/procedure/790
ORGANISME: One-Stop Shop — Investment Promotion Agency of Mauritania (APIM)
DATE_INDEXATION: 2026-05-17

Official summary as published on the national administrative procedures portal (Ijraati).

---

**Official title**: Création d'Entreprise (Business Creation)

**Users of the procedure**: Individuals, Professionals

**Entity in charge of the procedure**: One-Stop Shop — Investment Promotion Agency of Mauritania (APIM)

**Entity receiving the application**: One-Stop Shop

**Entity processing the application**: One-Stop Shop

**Processing time**: 48 hours

**Fees**:
- **Service free.**
- Only the **Commercial Court registry fees** are payable:
  - **200 MRU** for sole proprietorships (personnes physiques).
  - **500 MRU** for corporate entities (personnes morales).

**Required documents**: The file differs by legal form (sole proprietorship, corporate entity, GIE, branch). See the detailed per-form files in the Khidmaty knowledge base.

**Official contact**:
- Phone: +222 38 27 36 82
- Email: info-apim@apim.gov.mr
- Website: https://apim.gov.mr

**Legal basis**:
- Decree no. 2022-169 of 17 November 2022, on the creation of the unified business-creation application form (repeals Decree no. 2014-076 of 29 May 2014).
- Order no. 1487/MJ/2021 of 13 December 2021, defining the forms for applications to register in the local Commercial Registry and beneficial-owner declarations.
- Decree no. 2021-033 of 11 March 2021, on the Commercial Registry and Movable Securities Register.
- Law no. 2021-005 of 10 February 2021, amending Law no. 2000-005 of 18 January 2000 (Commercial Code).
- Law no. 2019-018 of 29 April 2019 establishing the General Tax Code (in force since 1 January 2020).
- Law no. 2021-007 of 22 February 2021, amending Law no. 67-039 of 3 February 1967 establishing the Social Security regime.
- Order no. 1974-116 of 17 September 1974, on terms for employer affiliation and contribution payments to the CNSS.
