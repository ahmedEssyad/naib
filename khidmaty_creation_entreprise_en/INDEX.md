# Pilot Domain — Business Creation (Khidmaty Voice — English)

**Focused, self-contained** knowledge base for voice-answering all common questions from a Mauritanian citizen or entrepreneur (or foreign investor) wishing to set up a business.

Full English translation of the original French version (`khidmaty_creation_entreprise/`).

## Consolidated sources

- **APIM** (Investment Promotion Agency of Mauritania) — "Business Setup" page and the official October 2025 brochure PDF.
- **procedures.gov.mr** (Ijraati portal) — official fiche #790.

## Translation notes

- Official legal terms (SARL, SA, GIE, CNSS, DGI, APIM, MRU) are kept in their original French form, as they appear that way in official documents — even in the English version.
- Mauritanian decree/law numbering is kept verbatim (e.g. "Decree no. 2022-169 of 17 November 2022") for traceability against the official Journal Officiel.
- "Khidmaty" (the official APIM registration platform) is distinguished throughout from "Khidmaty Voice" (the voice assistant being built).
