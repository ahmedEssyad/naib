## Opening hours

The official opening hours of the One-Stop Shop are **not published** on the APIM website or on procedures.gov.mr. Indicatively, Mauritanian public administrations generally open:

- **Sunday to Thursday**, from **8:00 AM to 4:00 PM**.
- Closed **Friday** and **Saturday** (Mauritanian weekend).
- Closed on national public holidays.

**Voice-assistant recommendation**: tell the user that exact opening hours must be confirmed by phone at +222 38 27 36 82 before any visit, especially for a first file.

## Khidmaty platform (online registration)

- This is the **official online registration portal** of the APIM One-Stop Shop.
- Not to be confused with **Khidmaty Voice**, the voice assistant that answers citizens' questions.
- Allows you to:
  - Download the unified form.
  - Submit your file without travelling.
  - Pay registry fees via mobile banking.
  - Track the status of your file.

## Paying registry fees — mobile banking

- Fees (200 or 500 MRU) are paid **only** via the mobile-banking accounts opened by the One-Stop Shop.
- Exact account references (numbers, operators: Bankily, Sedad, Masrvi, etc.) must be requested directly from the One-Stop Shop, from APIM, or on the Khidmaty platform at the time of submission.

## Why call the One-Stop Shop before going

- Confirm opening hours for the day.
- Check whether your activity requires a prior authorisation (regulated sectors).
- Ask for the exact mobile-banking payment procedure.
- Ask for the format of the unified form (paper or online).
- Verify the specific documents for your situation (foreigner, international GIE, branch, etc.).
