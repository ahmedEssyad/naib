# Overview — Setting Up a Business in Mauritania

SOURCE: https://apim.gov.mr/investor-services/business-setup/
SOURCE_OFFICIELLE: https://procedures.gov.mr/fr/procedure/790
ORGANISME: Investment Promotion Agency of Mauritania (APIM) — One-Stop Shop for Business Registration
DATE_INDEXATION: 2026-05-17

## In one sentence

Setting up a business in Mauritania is done at the **APIM One-Stop Shop** in **48 working hours**, provided the file is complete. The service is free; only the **Commercial Court registry fees** are payable (via mobile banking).

## What the One-Stop Shop does on your behalf

In a single step, APIM handles the three mandatory registrations for you:

1. **Commercial Registry** (at the Commercial Court)
2. **Tax Registration** (NIF — Tax Identification Number, at the DGI)
3. **Social Security Registration** (CNSS)

So you don't need to run around between the three administrations yourself.

## Official turnaround

**48 working hours** after a complete file is submitted. If a document is missing, the clock only starts once it's regularised.

## Cost

- **APIM service: free.**
- **Commercial Court registry fees**: payable via the mobile-banking accounts opened for this purpose.
  - Sole proprietorship: **200 MRU**
  - Corporate entity: **500 MRU**

## The four available legal forms

| Form | For whom | Minimum capital | Separate legal personality |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Sole proprietorship** (entreprise individuelle) | A single entrepreneur, commercial / industrial / artisanal activity | None | No — personal and business assets are merged |
| **Corporate entity** (SARL — LLC, SA — public limited company, cooperatives, etc.) | Multiple partners or shareholders wanting limited liability | Variable by form; mandatory declaration if > 10,000 MRU | Yes |
| **Economic Interest Group (GIE)** | Two or more persons (natural or legal) pooling resources without seeking their own profit | With or without capital | Yes |
| **Branch office** | A foreign company operating in Mauritania without creating a new entity | N/A — depends on the parent company | No — legally attached to the parent company |

## Online registration

Available via the **Khidmaty platform**: this is the official online registration portal of the APIM One-Stop Shop (not to be confused with **Khidmaty Voice**, the voice assistant).

## Who can register

- Mauritanian nationals wishing to launch their own business
- Companies (multiple partners, shared capital)
- Foreign investors (same rights as nationals, see FAQ)
- Foreign companies opening a branch in Mauritania
